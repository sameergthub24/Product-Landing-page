ABOUT US

At AppleHarv, our expertise spans decades in growing and selling premium apples. We understand the nuances of cultivating perfect apples, from the ideal appearance to optimal growing conditions. Apples thrive in temperate climates with loamy soil rich in organic matter, and precise drainage and aeration are crucial. Fortunately, our orchards are nestled in Kashmir, renowned as the "Paradise on Earth," which provides the perfect environment for temperate fruits. Kashmir’s unique soil, climate, and environment create an unparalleled setting for apple cultivation. This region's exceptional conditions endow our apples with a distinctive look, taste, flavor, size, and color, setting them apart on the global stage. At AppleHarvest, we harness these natural advantages to deliver apples that exemplify quality and excellence, making each bite a testament to the unique character of Kashmiri apples.
